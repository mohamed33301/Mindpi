Product Description

The NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin 32GB Developer Kit is a flagship AI computing platform designed for next-generation edge AI applications. Powered by an NVIDIA Ampere architecture GPU with 1792 CUDA cores and 56 Tensor Cores, it delivers up to 200 TOPS of AI performance for complex and real-time workloads.

Equipped with 32GB of high-bandwidth LPDDR5 memory and an 8-core Arm Cortex-A78AE CPU, the platform ensures fast data access, efficient multitasking, and smooth handling of large AI models. Its rich I/O support enables seamless integration with cameras, sensors, storage, and networking devices.

Running on NVIDIA JetPack SDK, the Jetson AGX Orin 32GB provides a complete AI software stack including CUDA, cuDNN, TensorRT, and DeepStream—making it ideal for rapid prototyping and deployment of professional AI systems.
